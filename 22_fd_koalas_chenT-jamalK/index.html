How to Use Foundation w/o Downloading Everything

Add this into the head of your HTML file & you are good to go!

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/foundation-sites@6.5.3/dist/css/foundation.min.css">

You can even use keystrokes! Ctrl+C or Cmd+C and Ctrl+V or Cmd+Cto copy and paste the link to the CDN
